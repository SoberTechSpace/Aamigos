AAMIGOS OBJECTIVES

Vision


Established as “The foundation that educates future generations about Maharashtrian culture and Ethos” and guided by Mr. Santosh Karande Sir, we are here to educate students and raise them as admirers of Marathi Culture. We are and will be a Globally established Online education school where students and families will be connected to our Maharashtrian Culture with knowledge.We will be the leading intellectual and creative center for Marathi culture for Students and families in a world who want to be connected to their roots of culture.

Mission


The ultimate aim of AAMIGOS is to provide an Education Platform for students who can discover, preserve, and transmit the knowledge, wisdom and values about Maharashtrian Culture to the future generations. AAMIGOS aims to help students to develop an understanding and appreciation for Maharashtrian Culture. The primary purpose of AAMIGOS is to provide a learning environment in which students can discover, test, conserve and transmit the knowledge, wisdom and values of Marathi Culture that will help to understand this and future generations.
