Marathi

The mother tongue of Maharashtrians. With over 98 million speakers of the language, Marathi has the 4th largest number of native speakers in the country. Barakhadi, Poems, Laghukatha(Short-stories) are the highlights of Marathi language. As a proud Maharashtrian, it is our responsibility to convey the importance of Marathi language to our future generations.
